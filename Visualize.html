Data Visualisation

Visualizing data helps to get a better understanding of the data.
Here is a dashboard/workbook representing the Monroe Crash data.

Doughnut chart : Collision-Type- Represents the count of crashs summarized by collision Types.
The collision types include: 2 cars, 3 cars, Bus , Cyclist , Motorcycle.

Doughnut chart : Year- Shows the count of crashes over the years.
Years : 2011,2012,2013,2014,2015,2016,2017

Piechart : Injury-Type- Represents the count of crashs summarized by Injury Types.
The Injury types include: Fatal,No-Injury,Incapacitating , Unknown .

Piechart : Crash Count by Hours- Represents the count of crashs occured across time.
